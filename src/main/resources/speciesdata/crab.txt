# Crab Species
# Crabs tend to scuttle sideways along boundaries and attack any enemy they encounter.
# This program simulates lateral movement and opportunistic infection.

# --- Attack if enemy encountered ---
ifenemy 1
infect 5
go 1

# --- If near a wall, turn right to follow the boundary ---
ifwall 3
right 90
go 1

# --- Simulate sideways movement ---
# If the space ahead is empty, turn left to simulate lateral movement,
# move forward a short distance, then turn right to reorient.
ifempty 4
left 90
move 10
right 90
go 1

# --- Otherwise, move forward ---
move 10
go 1
