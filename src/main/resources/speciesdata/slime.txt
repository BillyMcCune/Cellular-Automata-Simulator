# Slime Species
# Slime creatures ooze slowly, infecting any enemy that comes too close.
# When the path ahead is empty, they move forward at a slow pace.
# They also change direction when encountering a wall or randomly to mimic their amorphous nature.

# --- Attack Behavior ---
ifenemy 1
infect 3
go 1

# --- Slow Oozing Forward ---
ifempty 1
move 5
go 1

# --- Boundary Avoidance ---
ifwall 1
right 90
go 1

# --- Random Drift ---
ifrandom 1
left 45
go 1

# --- Default Movement ---
move 5
go 1
